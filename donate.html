O- : Universal Donor

O negative blood can be given to anyone! It's the most needed blood type for emergencies when there's no time to determine the patient's blood type.

Can donate to ALL blood types

AB+ : Universal Recipient

AB positive individuals can receive blood from any blood type! This makes them very flexible in emergency situations.

Can receive from ALL blood types

Every 2 seconds, someone in India needs blood

2

Only 37% of the population is eligible to donate blood

3

One unit of blood can save up to 3 lives

4

Blood cannot be manufactured – it can only come from donors

5

Your body replaces donated blood within 24-48 hours

6

Regular blood donation reduces harmful iron stores
